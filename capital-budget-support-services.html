The Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority (WMATA) provides Metrorail and Metrobus services throughout the greater Washington metropolitan area.  WMATA is the second largest rail transit system and the sixth largest bus network in the U.S.  It provided more than 301 Million trips throughout the Washington region in 2017.  WMATA contracted with a consultant team to evaluate its capital budgeting and funding processes and to identify improvements and alternative approaches to enhance WMATA’s ability to efficiently fund, finance, develop and report on capital program delivery consistent with program requirements. Cincar Consulting Group, LLC (C2G) is a sub-consultant to Infra-Strategies.

 

C2G is providing support to the following:

  • Reviewing and cataloging all funding agreements, board polices, and compliance requirements with a list of deliverables and schedules
  • Maintenance Planning and Asset Management
  • Optimization, Prioritization, and Alignment of Funding and Financing
  • Identification of Opportunities for Alternative Delivery and Public-Private Partnerships

 

C2G recently completed geocoding each of WMATA’s six Capital Investment Programs (CIP), which includes over 200 projects for Fiscal Years 2019 and 2020. The objective of this task was to create a geographic representation of the allocation of WMATA capital investment dollars for FY2019 and FY2020 and to provide an analysis of the resultant distributions of the capital investment by WMATA primary jurisdictions. Upcoming tasks will include funding, financial and economic impact analyses of the capital investment program.
