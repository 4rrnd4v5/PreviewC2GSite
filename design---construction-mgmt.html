Home  >  Services  >  Design & Construction Management

Cincar Consulting Group, LLC (C2G)'s experienced and dedicated staff work diligently with clients to provide engineering design services and construction management that fuse good planning, creativity, and innovation to produce sustainable, context-sensitive, and cost-effective results.

 

Our C2G designers are experienced in addressing client needs, community concerns, and project constraints associated with project development and scoping studies for roadways, highways, and multi-modal facilities. Our staff has developed plans and overseen early preliminary engineering through final design projects for local, state, and federal agencies, giving us in-depth knowledge of various protocols and procedures that are unique to different states and different agencies.   Understanding key risk elements in design, such as right-of-way, utilities, and environmental, help us to prioritize key design elements in a design schedule. Our experience designing projects across the country allows us to draw upon an expansive toolbox of resources while adhering to client-specific design guidelines.

 

Our C2G engineers and technicians are experienced in serving as extension of client staff to support the construction of projects on traditional delivery (design-bid-build), as well as innovative contracting. Our team members have both horizontal and vertical experience with transportation facilities and buildings.
