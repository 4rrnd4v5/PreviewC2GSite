DGS Capital Construction PMO – As sub consultant to ADC Management Solutions, C2G was retained in January 2018 to provide and independent assessment of the Office of Program Delivery (OPD) by the DC Department of General Services. Scope included a review of the organizational structure of the division and its capacity to lead and manage the DC Schools Modernization and Stabilization projects.

 

C2G staff were actively engage in:

  • Supporting technology for program delivery
  • Provide detailed status of each of the 12Modernization and 40 Stabilization projects that were scheduled to be delivered in August 2018

 

Following successful completion of the initial scope, we were retained to serve as Program Management/Delivery Strategic Advisor to help Office of Capital construction transition to new leadership, extend PM services to other portfolios within the division and lead a Tiger Team that was focused on ensuring that all future school projects are delivered on time. The Tiger Team performed construction management in depth review of each project and worked with A/E firms and contractors for each project to address all issues that would impede project timely completion.

 

C2G tasks include:

  • Supporting the Capital Project Office to refine organization chart, key position descriptions.
  • Enhancing implementation strategies
  • Updating/integrating project delivery manual with new project management software platform
