Home  >  Projects & Clients  >  Projects  >  M-59 from Romeo Plank to I-94

Cincar Consulting Group, LLC (C2G) worked as a subconsultant on the full Construction Engineering Services for the M-59 Project from Romeo Plank Road to I-94.  The Construction Engineering services for the prime included: project administration; meeting coordination and attendance; inspection; staking, quality assurance testing and reporting; measurement, computation, and documentation of quantities; reporting and record keeping; processing progress pay estimates, contract modifications, PHD, and all other contract documentation; and finalizing all project documentation.

 

C2G served as a Mentor Protégé in the Construction Services – Office Technician role working for the prime through February 2022, the project will be completed in 2023.

 

C2G provided support to the following:

  • Office Technician
  • Field Manager
