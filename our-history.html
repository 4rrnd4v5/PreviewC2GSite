Our History

Cincar Consulting Group, LLC (C2G), is a DBE certified firm in Georgia, Michigan, North Carolina, Florida, Maryland and District of Columbia, with highly experienced consultants dedicated to helping organizations deploy their resources more strategically to create and manage infrastructure assets that transform communities.

 

C2G was founded to assist and partner with clients in realizing their vision, accomplishing their goals and maximizing the effectiveness of their resources. We provide forward looking ideas by listening and then designing creative solutions.  We address your needs to help you create your success.

A Single Focus.  A Single Team.  Together, we provide scalable strategic insights that foster a sustainable and equitable future.
