Home  >  Services  >  Planning

Cincar Consulting Group, LLC (C2G) partners with clients to deliver cost effective, safe, and quality infrastructure assets. Our mission is to support successful delivery through all project stages of planning, design, and construction. C2G key areas of support involve program and project delivery, controls, stakeholder engagement/ communication, and construction oversight.

 

C2G supports our clients with the needed professional resources to deliver. C2G assists clients with an overall assessment of project delivery from organizational to delivery aspects, including review/development of project delivery processes, tools, delivery manuals, and training. C2G staff are well versed in development of project management plans, identifying, and mitigating risks, and establishing appropriate quality and financial plans that meet the project’s needs. C2G also supports our clients with extension of staff services for projects and/or programs.

 

Communications of large projects and programs are crucial both internally and externally for success. C2G is experienced in supporting both internal and external communications with developing plans to address communications protocols, social media and website content, newsletters, and surveys.

 

Construction services include oversight, verification, and inspection to support project delivery.

Fayetteville Area

Transit Authority TDP

 

Fayetteville Area System of Transit (FAST) is the primary transit provider for the City of Fayetteville. It operates a network of 18 fixed routes, as well as a para-transit service. The service area encompasses 95 square miles and has a service area population of approximately 150,300.

Dekalb County

Transit Master Plan

 

Cincar Consulting Group, LLC (C2G) was part of the consultant team retained to develop a transit master plan for DeKalb County, Georgia, in partnership with the Atlanta Regional Commission (ARC) and MARTA. The plan is a 30-year vision for future transit investments in DeKalb County. It addresses the mobility challenges and opportunities, economic development, and improvements to enhance quality of life for DeKalb County residents.

Public Involvement &

Community Studies Staff Support

 

Cincar Consulting Group (C2G) is providing staff support to NCDOT’s Environmental Analysis Unit, specifically the Public Involvement, Community Studies and Visualization (PICSViz) Group. Work includes the review of Community Studies draft reports and the Community Studies portion of draft environmental documents produced by consulting firms working for NCDOT.
