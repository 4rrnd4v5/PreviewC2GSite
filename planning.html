Planning

Cincar Consulting Group, LLC (C2G), our planners work with communities and clients to develop solutions that enhance livability in communities that are equitable, inclusive, resilient, and well-connected. Helping our clients imagine the future is the key focus of our planning practice. We offer expertise in strategy development; NEPA analysis and documentation; transportation planning, equitable development; and community engagement. We never forget that we are planning for people first. We recognize the critical connections between transportation, land use, livability, and equity. We use our extensive and unique skills to create great places for people to live, work, learn, and play.

 

Our strong relationships with resource agencies and key federal, state, and local stakeholders in the communities where we work give clients confidence that we can identify challenges early and know who to coordinate with and the information needed to address then in a timely manner. We collaborate closely with our clients to ensure plans are feasible, cost-effective, and implementable. We structure our planning process around a community engagement strategy tailored for each individual project to ensure plans respect the community’s past and reflect its vision for the future.
